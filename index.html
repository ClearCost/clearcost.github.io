How it works

The Clear Cost calculator estimates the original price of goods before tariffs. Enter the price including tariff, select the import country and item type, and our calculator applies typical tariff rates to provide an estimate.

For example, if you enter a price of $100 for a tech item imported from China, the calculator will apply a 20% tariff rate, estimating the original price to be $83.33.

Common Questions

What is a tariff?

A tariff is a tax imposed by governments on imported goods, increasing their price. Clear Cost helps you calculate the original price before this tax.

How does the calculator work?

Enter the price including tariff, select the import country and item type, and our calculator estimates the original price by applying typical tariff rates.

Are the results accurate?

Our calculator uses average tariff rates for estimates. For precise rates, consult official trade regulations.

Can I calculate tariffs for any country?

Currently, we support major trading partners like the EU, China, and Canada. Select “Other” for general estimates or reach out for custom calculations.
