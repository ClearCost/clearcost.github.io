Clear Cost

"See the Real Price."

Clear Cost is a financial solutions company that provides honest pricing for international trade. Our Clear Cost calculator shows the true price of goods before tariffs – something other businesses like Amazon hide. We believe in transparency, helping people see the real cost of what they buy. Simply put, we're against hidden fees and overpriced imports.

How It Works

  1. Enter the Price: Start by entering the total price of your item, including tariffs.
  2. Select the Country: Choose the country from which the item is being imported.
  3. Enter the Item Type: Specify the type of item you're importing, as tariffs can vary based on the product category.
  4. Get the Price Without Tariffs: Our tool will automatically calculate and display the price of your item before tariffs were added, giving you a clear breakdown of the costs.
